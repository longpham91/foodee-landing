Features

It’s all about the food!

With Foodee, users will have the unique opportunity to rate and review specific dishes- so it’s not just about restaurants.

It all comes down to Taste, Aroma, Flavor and Presentation.

Nothing else really matters.

It’s food photography!

With Foodee, users can easily capture their mouth-watering food photos and share them with the world.

What is better than an image of a hot chocolate cup at your local coffee shop or a photo of a juicy hamburger at a restaurant in the city you are visiting?

You can’t go wrong with food or photography or both!

Sharing is caring!

Nothing brings people together like good food.

Try something good, share the pleasure with the world. Love the dish you see in a photo posted by another foodie, “pin” it to try for later.

Good food is better when shared with good friends.
