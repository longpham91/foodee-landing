About Us

Foodee is a fun and informative community where foodies around the world can easily connect, create memories, and share their dining experiences via photos.

Foodee’s focus is on the food and not the rating of the restaurant. You may be a grade C restaurant but that doesn’t mean you don’t have grade A dishes. For restaurants, our app will let your best dishes shine through so foodies can enjoy them.

Sneak Peek

It’s all about the food!

With Foodee, you will have the unique opportunity to rate and review specific dishes - so it’s not just about restaurants.

It all comes down to Taste, Aroma, Flavor and Presentation.

Nothing else really matters.

It’s all about social-networking!

With Foodee, you can easily learn what’s good and what’s popular right now by exploring our daily updated feed with stunning food photos posted by other users along with reviews and ratings.

You can also quickly customize search results by utilizing our filtering and sorting options to fully satisfy your cravings.

Be Full. Be Creative.

With Foodee, you can freely create and customize your own food collections. See something good, “pin” it down to try for later.
